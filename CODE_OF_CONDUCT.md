# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We expect all participants to be respectful and constructive.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or discriminatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others’ private information without consent
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit content, or temporarily or permanently block contributors who do not follow this Code of Conduct.

## Contact

Report concerns by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers privately if preferred.
